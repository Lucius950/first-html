Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is creamy, savory, and incredibly easy to make. It's perfect for a quick dinner but fancy enough to impress guests!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While it cooks, fry the pancetta or bacon until crisp.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with the Parmesan.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and return it to the pot.
  5. Quickly stir in the pancetta and egg-cheese mixture while the pasta is hot.
  6. Mix thoroughly, season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.
